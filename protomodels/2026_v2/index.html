ProtoModels, 2026 Production

In our previous work, we presented a prototype algorithm that was designed to learn a precursor theory to a tentative Next Standard Model. In this update we bring the idea to full fruition. The algorithm has received significant upgrades to all its components. The input data has grown significantly. Running on the actual observations, we identify a protomodel with a chargino-like particle and three neutralino-like particles as our best performing result. The $p$-value of this protomodel has been very approximately found to be ~ 0.01.
